Color Logic

Light mode uses darker gold (#B8860B) as primary for contrast on white. Dark mode flips to brighter gold (#DEBD4E) for readability on dark backgrounds. The warm cream/dark gold accent surfaces give sections visual variety without introducing a second brand color.
